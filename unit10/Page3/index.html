Kraft Mac & Cheese 2 boxes
Butter 1 stick
Milk 1/2 cup
Pepperoni 1 cup chopped
Hot Sauce your preference
Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese 1 1/2 cups
Pepperoni 20 slices
Shredded Mozzerella Cheese 2 cups
Pre heat oven to 400 degrees. Boil water and add 2 boxes of Mac and Cheese. Bring water back to boil and boil for 7 minutes. Drain water. Add drained Mac and Cheese back into pot with 1 stick butter, 1/2 cup milk, 1 cup chopped pepperoni, 1 1/2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese and your preffered amount of your favorite hot sauce. Once all mixed together, add to greased glass baking dish. Top with pepperoni slices and shredded mozzerella cheese. Bake for your 25 minutes or until mozzerella cheese is starting to brown. Take out of oven and let cool for 5 to 10 minutes before serving.
